Trajectory lines

A trajectory lines data object stores the continuous motion paths of a set of particles, which have been precomputed by a Generate trajectory lines modifier for the purpose of visualization.

The visual appearance of the trajectory lines in rendered images is controlled through the associated Trajectory lines visual element.
